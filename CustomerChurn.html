Bank Customer Churn Prediction

  • Category: Machine Learning
  • Client: Personal(worked as a collaborator)
  • Project date:Feb, 2024

Summary

  • Objective: Analyze customer demographics and financial information to predict whether a customer will leave the bank.

  • Dataset: Kaggle dataset with 10,000 rows and 14 columns, including customer age, gender, country, credit score, balance, and more.

  • Key Factors Influencing Churn: Age, Geography, Tenure, Balance, Number of Products, Credit Card status, Active Membership status.

  • Models & Performance: Random Forest Classifier: Achieved best results with an accuracy score of 0.861 and better precision compared to Decision Tree Classifier.

  • Conclusion: Identified significant factors influencing customer churn, with Random Forest Classifier providing the most accurate predictions.
