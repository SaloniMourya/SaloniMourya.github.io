Data Science Jobs Salaries Dashboard

  • Category: Tableau Dashboard
  • Client: Academic
  • Project date: April, 2024
  • Dashboard URL: Data Science Jobs Salaries Dashboard
  • Dashboard Story URL: Data Science Jobs Salaries Dashboard Story

Summary

  • Objective: Analyze and visualize data science job salaries to provide insights into salary trends based on job title, experience level, employment type, and geographical location.

  • Data Preprocessing: Cleaned and standardized data by transforming aliases for experience levels (e.g., EN to Entry Level), employment types (e.g., FT to Full Time), and company sizes (e.g., L to Large) to enhance clarity.

  • Key Performance Indicators (KPIs): Average salary by country, job title, and experience level; distribution of companies by size and location; top employee residences.

  • Findings:
    Identified countries with the highest average data science salaries.
    Revealed salary variations across different experience levels and job types.
    Highlighted the top 10 locations where data scientists reside.

  • Dashboard Features:
    Interactive filters for location and job title to customize data exploration.
    Visualizations including pie charts, bar charts, maps, and scatter plots for clear and actionable insights.
